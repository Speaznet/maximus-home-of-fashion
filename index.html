Our Story

Experience Fashion Beyond Style

Born in the cultural heart of Ibadan and led by master designer Ogunyemi Abiodun, Maximus Home of Fashion is a luxury house dedicated to redefining modern African couture.

Every garment is a conversation between heritage and innovation — precision tailoring, hand-finished detailing, and fabrics that move like poetry. We dress grooms, brides, executives and modern style icons who refuse to be ordinary.

The Designer

Where Tradition Meets Tailoring Mastery

At the helm of Maximus Home of Fashion is Ogunyemi Abiodun — a fashion design specialist whose signature lies in bespoke men's wear and contemporary African couture. From a small atelier near the University of Ibadan, his vision has grown into one of Nigeria's most respected luxury houses.

Every stitch is intentional. Every silhouette, sculpted. Every client — treated like royalty. This is not fashion. This is legacy.

— Ogunyemi Abiodun, Creative Director

Frequently Asked

Answers, Elegantly Served

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timelines depend on design complexity, order volume and event date. Use the booking form or WhatsApp to confirm the current production schedule for your project.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Delivery and dispatch options can be confirmed during consultation. Add your preferred destination when booking so the team can advise the best fulfillment option.

Do you accept urgent orders?

Urgent orders are reviewed based on workshop availability and the complexity of the requested outfit. Contact the brand directly to confirm whether your date can be accommodated.

How do fittings work?

Fittings can be arranged around your location and order type. The brand can guide you through measurements during consultation if you are not visiting physically.

How can clients contact support?

You can reach the brand by phone, WhatsApp, the booking form on this page, and official social media links once connected.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes. The booking form includes a consultation-type selector so virtual consultations can be requested directly from the website.

Are training classes available online?

Training availability can be presented as online, physical or blended classes. Update the academy section and booking form as your current schedule changes.
